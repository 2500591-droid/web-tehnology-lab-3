University Programs Overview

ADCS (Associate Degree in Computer Science)

Duration: 2 years

Focus: Fundamental programming, database basics, and introductory computer science concepts.

Career: Suitable for entry-level IT roles or as a foundation for further studies.

BSCS (Bachelor of Science in Computer Science)

Duration: 4 years

Focus: In-depth programming, algorithms, software development, networking, and advanced computer science topics.

Career: Prepares students for professional software development, research, or higher studies in computer science.
